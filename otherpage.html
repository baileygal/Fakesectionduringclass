Panigacci

Aperitif • Dinner • Cocktail Bar Much more than a Bistro:
in the wake of the historic Lunigiana sandwich-based restaurant, a new concept of a multi-purpose venue is born which ranges from the aperitif,
to the small restaurant with sung dinner or à la carte to the cocktail bar. An unconventional place to feel at home. Point of reference for a young
but demanding clientele and for those looking for an "IN" place to spend an evening or celebrate an important event.

Pyt Milano

PYT Milano is an exclusive restaurant where you can enjoy dinner shows every evening worthy of being remembered and told.
Shows with which to transform a simple dinner into an experience capable of involving and inebriating all five senses,
immersive experiences capable of making you forget the stress of everyday life, eclectic dimensions with which to enter a new reality.
An à la carte menu, a selection of bottles capable of giving you true sensorial experiences, always different shows with exceptional performers.
PYT is the dining place where every desire to escape from reality is realized: an exclusive place, a place in which to live the most EXTRAORDINARY EXPERIENCE.

Cantine Milano

Cantine Milano is the first and only Italian Wine Restaurant where the experience begins with a passionate choice of wine, and only then is paired
with the perfect food! Our menu is a real sensorial journey that explores innovative pairings, with wine guiding every gastronomic choice.
The cuisine, inspired by the Mediterranean tradition, is revisited in a gourmet key to enhance every sip and every dish. Every visit to Cantine Milano promises to be an
extraordinary culinary experience, where wine is not just an accompaniment, but the absolute protagonist.
